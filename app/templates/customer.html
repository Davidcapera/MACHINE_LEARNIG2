Customer Spending Prediction

Prediction of customer spending using a simple regression model.

Case Use Overview
Title

Customer spending prediction using a linear formula

Problem Description

Companies need to estimate how much a customer will spend. This helps marketing, sales, and customer segmentation. The model uses age, income, and number of visits to predict spending.

Type of Machine Learning
Supervised Learning Regression
Input Features
  • Age
  • Income
  • Visits
Expected Output

Predicted customer spending value

Real world relevance

Used in marketing analysis, customer behavior prediction, and business intelligence.

Training Data
Age Income Visits Spending
20 1000 2 84
30 2000 5 170
40 4000 10 360
Model Formula

Spending = 0.1 * Income + 2 * Visits − Age
