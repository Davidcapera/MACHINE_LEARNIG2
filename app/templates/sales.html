Sales Forecasting - Machine Learning

Problem Description

Companies need to predict product sales based on marketing investment and product pricing. Machine learning helps identify relationships between advertising budget, product price, and expected sales.

Machine Learning Type

Supervised Learning — Linear Regression

Variables
  • Advertising Budget (Independent Variable)
  • Product Price (Independent Variable)
  • Market Demand Index (Independent Variable)
  • Sales (Dependent Variable)
Dataset Description

This dataset represents simulated business data used for sales forecasting. The model learns the relationship between advertising investment, product price, market demand, and total sales.

  • Advertising Budget — Marketing investment amount
  • Product Price — Price per unit
  • Demand Index — Market demand indicator
  • Sales — Total units sold

Regression Model

Sales = b + m₁(Advertising) + m₂(Price) + m₃(Demand Index)

Model Explanation

This model uses Multiple Linear Regression to estimate sales. The algorithm learns the relationship between advertising investment, product price, and demand index to predict expected sales values.

Multiple Linear Regression model representation for sales prediction

Interpretation

The model estimates sales based on marketing investment, product pricing, and market demand. Increasing advertising and demand typically increases sales, while increasing price may reduce sales.
