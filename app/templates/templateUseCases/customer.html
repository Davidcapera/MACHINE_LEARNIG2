Case Use Overview

Machine learning allows companies to analyze customer data and estimate future behavior using mathematical models. This type of prediction is essential in modern business analytics and customer intelligence.

Problem Description

Companies need to estimate how much a customer will spend. This helps marketing, sales, and customer segmentation.

Input Features
1 Age

Customer's age in years. Affects spending patterns and product preferences.

2 Income

Monthly income in dollars. Higher income correlates with higher spending capacity.

3 Visits

Monthly store or platform visits. Frequency is a strong engagement signal.

Output: Predicted spending — a numeric value representing how much the customer is expected to spend.
Training Data

Model Formula
Spending =
0.1 × Income
+ 2 × Visits
− 1 × Age
Each variable has a coefficient that weights its influence. Income and Visits are positive; Age has a negative effect in this model.
