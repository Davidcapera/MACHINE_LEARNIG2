Air Quality Prediction

This system predicts air quality levels based on pollution particles (PM2.5 and PM10). It simulates a machine learning model using rule-based decision logic.

How it works:
The model evaluates pollution levels and classifies air quality into four categories: Good, Moderate, Unhealthy, and Hazardous.
